## The Joy of Missing Out (JOMO)

JOMO is the conscious choice to step away from the noise, pressure, and constant comparison of modern life—especially driven by social media—and instead focus on what truly matters.

Rather than fearing what you might miss (FOMO), JOMO is about being present, intentional, and content with your own path. It means valuing real experiences over digital ones, protecting your time and energy, and finding happiness in simplicity.

In short:  
**JOMO is not missing out—it's choosing better.**
